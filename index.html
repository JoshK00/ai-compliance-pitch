The EU AI Act is live — and constantly evolving. Tracking every amendment manually is hard at scale.
02 / ACCOUNTABILITY
Companies must prove their AI systems are responsible. Without tooling, this means expensive legal teams and slow audits.

Manual compliance checks are slow, error-prone, and cost-prohibitive — especially for mid-market companies.

Non-compliance penalties under the EU AI Act reach up to €35M or 7% of global revenue. The stakes are existential.
